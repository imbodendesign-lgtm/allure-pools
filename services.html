Common Questions

How much does weekly pool service cost in Las Vegas?
Regular pool maintenance is $160 per month for four visits — chemical balancing, netting, leaf baskets and waterline tile brushing — with no long-term contract.
Do you require a contract?
No. Service is month-to-month with a simple 30-day cancellation notice. We earn your business every month.
What areas does Allure Pools serve?
We serve Henderson, Las Vegas and Boulder City across the entire Las Vegas valley.
How often should I clean my pool filter?
We recommend a deep filter cleaning twice a year — typically January and July — to protect your equipment and keep circulation flawless. Filter cleaning is $75.
Who actually services my pool?
Owner Douglas Nandi personally oversees every account, bringing more than 20 years of hands-on experience to each visit. When you call, you reach Douglas — and he answers.
